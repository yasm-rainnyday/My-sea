My dear sea

I want to show my appreciation yet once again, from dusk...

To dawn...

The dark has come
Slowly settling in slumber
A deep blue shade of color
A shade that i deeply love
Just simply in awe of
The quiet moments after all the light of the day
Reminds me how much i want to stay
Here forever with you , my dear sea....

Deep in the sea

You and i are just like jellyfishes
Endulging in our sillyness
You are the Cosmina , i am your Nicoleta
......
and untill death
You are the sea and i am your earth
I love you my platonic valentine
